Co-Vast


Expand intelligence to autonomous collaboration, converging on human interaction

An open source simulation platform for autonomous driving and autonomous vehicle collaboration

Getting Started Demo Video(BiliBili)

More realistic communication simulation

Co-Vast is based on the spatial index, considering the real-time location and communication range of the vehicle agent (VA), simulating the communication challenges in reality, while achieving low-latency and non-inductive communication

Efficient Autonomous Collaboration

Based on V-SANet, Co-Vast realizes the independent establishment of collaboration between VA and the selective fusion of data, providing a more realistic solution. Facing the present, aiming at the future

What You See Is What You Get

Co-Vast’s VA module supports processing data from sensors. After training and testing in the Field, it can be directly deployed on physical hardware without modification. What you see is what you get
